## SEO Strategy Recommendations:

### High-Priority Topics (Start with these)
1. "Lower Back Pain: Exercises and Treatment"
2. "What to Expect at Your First Physiotherapy Appointment"
3. "Sciatica Pain Relief: Complete Treatment Guide"
4. "Carpal Tunnel Syndrome: Prevention and Treatment"
5. "Running Injuries: Prevention and Recovery"

### Content Clusters to Build
- **Back Pain Hub**: Group related articles (lower back, sciatica, herniated disc, posture)
- **Sports Injury Hub**: Collection of sports-specific injury articles
- **Workplace Health Hub**: Ergonomics and RSI-related content
- **Recovery Hub**: Post-surgical rehabilitation guides

### SEO Best Practices for Each Blog Post:
1. **Target long-tail keywords** (e.g., "how to treat lower back pain at home" vs. "back pain")
2. **Create comprehensive guides** (2000-3000 words for authority)
3. **Include internal links** to services and other blog posts
4. **Use descriptive headers** (H1, H2, H3 with keywords)
5. **Add meta descriptions** under 160 characters
6. **Include schema markup** for FAQ, HowTo, and Article types
7. **Optimize images** with alt text describing the content
8. **Use related keywords** in image filenames
9. **Create a table of contents** for long-form content
10. **Add a CTA** linking to contact form or appointment booking
